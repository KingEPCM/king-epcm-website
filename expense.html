Expenses & reimbursements

Claim back business expenses, mileage, and PEO/PEng professional fees. Pick a type below and fill it in — when you submit, it's sent automatically (with your receipts) to the right team: mileage and expenses go to Accounting, and PEO/PEng fees go to HR. You're reimbursed with your next pay run.

How it works

  1. Check "Your recent claims" first. A quick look helps you avoid submitting the same thing twice.
  2. Choose what you're claiming — Mileage, an Expense claim, or PEO/PEng fees — then add a line for each item. Totals add up for you.
  3. Attach a receipt to every expense and professional-fee item. A clear photo or PDF is fine (each under 4 MB). Mileage is distance-based, so no receipt is needed — if you have parking or toll receipts, claim them as an Expense item so the receipt is attached.
  4. Click "Submit." Your claim and receipts are sent automatically — expenses and mileage to Accounting, PEO/PEng fees to HR. No email to open or press send. You'll see an on-screen confirmation.
  5. You'll be reimbursed through payroll once it's processed, usually with the next pay run.

Good to know: business travel, project purchases, client meals, and approved supplies generally qualify — ask your manager if you're unsure. PEO/PEng fees are covered up to $500 per calendar year. Mileage is paid at the current company rate (HR applies it). Submitting promptly keeps things easy while receipts are fresh.
