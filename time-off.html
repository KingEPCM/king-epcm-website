Time off & vacation

How to request planned time off in QuickBooks Workforce, and what to do for emergencies.

🚨 Emergency or sick day?

If you're sick, have a family emergency, or can't make it in on short notice, there are two steps. First, email HR right away so your absence is recorded and coverage can be arranged — don't wait. Then log it in QuickBooks Workforce as soon as you're able. Even if you can't submit Workforce in the moment, it still has to be entered there for payroll and your records.

✉️ 1. Email HR now 🏖️ 2. Log it in Workforce

Also message your project lead or manager directly so they're aware. The Workforce entry is still required — don't skip it once you're back.

Planned time off — request in QuickBooks Workforce

Vacation and other planned time off is requested and approved in QuickBooks Workforce. Once approved it syncs to payroll automatically. Submit your request as early as you can so your manager has time to approve it.

  1. Sign in to Workforce. Go to workforce.intuit.com (or open the QuickBooks Workforce mobile app) and sign in with your Intuit work account.
  2. Open the Time off section. From the main menu, select Time off. Here you can see your available balances (vacation, personal, etc.).
  3. Start a new request. Select Request time off.
  4. Choose the type. Pick the policy that applies — for example Vacation or Personal.
  5. Select your dates. Enter the start and end dates. For a partial day, enter the number of hours instead of a full day.
  6. Add a note (optional). Include any context that helps your manager approve it.
  7. Submit. Your manager is notified and reviews the request. You'll get a notification when it's approved or declined.
