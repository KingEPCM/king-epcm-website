    About King EPCM

    Flexible, dependable, on-site engineering — built for the realities of land development.

    Our story

    Established in 2019, grounded in the field

    King EPCM was established in 2019 to provide essential civil and mining engineering services, built on a simple promise: Flexible, Dependable, On-site Engineering.

    We deliver the full range of engineering services required for land development across the Golden Horseshoe, from our office in the Markham / Stouffville area of Ontario. We also provide mining and aggregate engineering services throughout Canada.

    We understand that clients are busy and projects often run on complicated schedules. That's why we offer unsurpassed flexibility in supporting our clients — we're here to help your project, wherever and whenever required.

    Our philosophy

    Three commitments behind every project

    FFlexible

    We adapt to your timelines, site conditions, and changing requirements — meeting the project where it is.

    DDependable

    Timely, accurate, and consistent work you can rely on through planning, permitting, and construction.

    OOn-site

    We engineer from the ground, not just the desk — present in the field where the real conditions are.

    Where we work

    Serving Ontario, and Canada-wide for mining

    Our civil, environmental, and geotechnical services cover the Greater Toronto Area and the Golden Horseshoe. Our mining and aggregate engineering services extend across all of Canada.
