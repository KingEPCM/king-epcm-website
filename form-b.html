Self-assessment Form B

About 15 minutes. Score yourself honestly — this is to make your review conversation useful, not to catch anyone out. Completing as you.

Performance Review · Form version V1 – 2026-06-10

Before you start

Eight questions (four Effectiveness, four Company Fit), each scored 1–5, plus a few short notes. ~15 min. Score honestly — this is to make your review conversation useful, not to catch anyone out.

Scoring guide — how to score 1–5 (tap to open)

The 1–5 scale

1Well below standard — can't be relied on without redoing it
2Below standard — needs significant support or correction
3Meets the basic bar — acceptable, not yet moving forward
4Strong — consistently meets the standard on your own
5Excellent — exceeds it and raises the bar for others

Effectiveness — the four measures

MeasureThe question
ImprovementAre you making yourself and your teammates better?
Systems & methodsAre you improving how the work gets done, not just doing it?
ProductivityIs the output usable and delivered on time?
QualityDoes the work meet or exceed the standard, first-time-right?

Company Fit — the four core values

ValueWhat it means
IndependenceDecide, troubleshoot, and finish without being carried
TeamworkWork well with everyone — including difficult people
Active listeningCatch key points; grasp problems people can't quite name
Expectation managementManage expectations both internal and external
