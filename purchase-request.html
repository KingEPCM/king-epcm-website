Purchase request

For equipment, supplies, software, and subscriptions. Read the steps, then submit the form below.

💡 Small purchases under $50

For anything under $50 you're good to go ahead with the purchase — no approval form needed. Just make sure you let your manager (or the related personnel) and accounting know so it's recorded and charged to the right project. For purchases $50 and over, submit the request below for approval first.

Before you submit

  1. Submit before you buy. A purchase request is for approval before the company buys something. If you already paid out of pocket, use an expense reimbursement instead.
  2. Describe what you need. Include the item, quantity, and a link or quote if you have one.
  3. Add the estimated cost and which project or department it's for.
  4. Explain why. A short note on why it's needed helps your manager approve it faster.
  5. Submit the form below. It routes to your manager and operations for approval. You'll be notified once it's approved and ordered.

For larger or recurring purchases, check with your manager first — some items may need additional sign-off.
