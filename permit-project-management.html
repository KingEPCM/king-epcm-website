    Permit & Project Management

    Regulatory approvals, agency coordination, and project delivery from concept to completion.

    Management

    What we do

    We manage permit approvals, agency coordination, and project delivery from concept to completion — keeping your project on track, on budget, and fully compliant across residential subdivisions, infrastructure, and commercial developments.

    Septic Permit

    Septic permit application services including site evaluation, system design, and coordination with local health authorities — a smooth approval process that meets all regulatory requirements for safe and effective wastewater management.

    Site Alteration Permit

    Site alteration permit services for grading, drainage, and land development projects. We prepare the required drawings and manage submissions to ensure efficient approvals and full compliance.

    ECA / MECP / PTTW

    ECA and PTTW application services supporting wastewater, stormwater, and water-taking approvals. We prepare technical documentation and coordinate with regulatory agencies for efficient approvals and full environmental compliance.

    Questions & answers

    Permit & Project Management — FAQs

    What permits can King EPCM help me obtain?

    We assist with Environmental Compliance Approvals (ECA), Permit to Take Water (PTTW), septic permits, site-alteration permits, and building and site-plan approvals.

    What does King EPCM's project management include?

    We coordinate approvals, agencies, and engineering disciplines, and manage delivery from planning and design through execution and approval.

    How long do approvals and permits take?

    Timelines vary by municipality, agency, and permit type. We help anticipate requirements early so that submissions are complete and avoidable delays are minimized.
