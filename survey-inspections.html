    Survey & Inspections

    Topographic surveys plus asphalt and concrete construction QA/QC.

    Field services

    What we do

    Accurate field data and on-site oversight that keep design grounded in real conditions and construction true to design — from scouting raw land to quality-controlling materials during construction.

    Topographic Survey

    Our experienced outdoor survey crew scouts the land and has data ready before detailed planning takes place — defining the features of a property and uncovering its real worth beneath the brush. Legal surveying is offered through dedicated OLS partners.

    Asphalt QA/QC

    Asphalt must be properly managed during placement to ensure a long service life. Our program includes in-situ native material review, base/sub-base review & design, asphalt extraction, gradation and mix design, the full Marshall test, on-site void hot-compaction testing, and Theoretical Maximum Density testing.

    Concrete & Formworks QA/QC

    Concrete is the literal foundation of modern buildings. Our QA/QC program includes slump tests, air entrainment %, concrete cylinder strength testing, rebar and formworks review, and vibration and concrete placement oversight.
