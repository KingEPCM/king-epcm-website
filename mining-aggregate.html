Mining & Aggregate

Open pit and underground engineering services. By miners, for miners.

Canada-wide

What we do

King EPCM offers a full range of mining and aggregate engineering services for junior miners and senior operators across Canada, backed by licensed P.Eng's and P.Geo's with significant technical and operational experience.

Professional Services

Regional geology review, core logging, modelling of geological structures, orebody interpretation, 3D borehole logging & orebody estimation, Indicated/Inferred/Resource/Reserve statements, and preliminary extraction sequence and feasibility review.

Financial Services

Fully NI 43-101 compliant consulting with multiple Qualified Persons — discounted cashflow models for concept selection, Preliminary Economic Assessment (PEA), Prefeasibility and Bankable Feasibility Studies, and WACC/IRR and borrowing-cost review.

Underground Mining

Operational underground mining engineering — drill, blast and cable bolt planning, short- and long-range planning, drill letters, blast & loading prints, field directions and memos, and Standard Operating Procedures.

Specialized Underground Engineering

Paste fill operations and wall design, continuous backfill optimization, digital/analog wall pressure sensors, ventilation design and VentSim modelling, and chemical environmental sampling.

Open Pit — Metals Mining

Background geological verification, preliminary feasibility ore extraction sequence review, detailed open-pit design and extraction sequencing, ore/waste optimization with cashflow sequencing, and equipment and financial due diligence.

Open Pit — Quarry

Urban aggregate quarry (limestone) and pit (sand & gravel) design, Aggregate Resources Act (ARA) of Ontario applications, financial calculations, equipment sizing and operations improvements, and geological verification.
