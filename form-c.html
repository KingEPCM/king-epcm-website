Manager assessment Form C

Your independent read on a team member. Score on your own first — don't open their Form B until you've formed your own view, then compare in the conversation.

Performance Review · Form version V1 – 2026-06-10

Managers only

This form is for managers completing a review of a team member. If you should have access, email it@kingepcm.com to be added as a manager.

Before you start

Score the four Effectiveness measures and four Company Fit values 1–5, with a line of evidence for each set, then set the outcome. Same 1–5 scale as the self-assessment — open the guide below if you need it.

Scoring guide — how to score 1–5 (tap to open)

The 1–5 scale

1Well below standard — can't be relied on without redoing it
2Below standard — needs significant support or correction
3Meets the basic bar — acceptable, not yet moving forward
4Strong — consistently meets the standard on their own
5Excellent — exceeds it and raises the bar for others

Effectiveness — the four measures

MeasureThe question
ImprovementAre they making themselves and teammates better?
Systems & methodsAre they improving how the work gets done, not just doing it?
ProductivityIs the output usable and delivered on time?
QualityDoes the work meet or exceed the standard, first-time-right?

Company Fit — the four core values

ValueWhat it means
IndependenceDecides, troubleshoots, and finishes without being carried
TeamworkWorks well with everyone — including difficult people
Active listeningCatches key points; grasps problems people can't quite name
Expectation managementManages expectations both internal and external
