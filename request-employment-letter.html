Request an employment letter

Need proof of employment or income — for a mortgage, rental, loan, or visa? Send HR a request and we'll prepare a signed letter for you.

How it works

  1. Send your request below. It's sent to HR automatically from your account — add a note if there's anything specific the letter needs to mention (the recipient, salary vs. employment-only, a deadline, etc.).
  2. HR prepares the letter. We confirm your details and generate an official, signed King EPCM letter.
  3. Turnaround is about 2–3 business days. If it's urgent, mention your deadline in the comments and we'll do our best.
